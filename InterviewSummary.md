Context

Goal
Give me a structured summary of my candidate from my interview notes 

Output Format 
Your output should be straightforward, but more for structuring and proper sentence for my hiring manager to read and review with ease
It should be unbiased without making the candidate look too bad, but can't be overselling the candidate as well 
The tone should be friendly, humanistic and sound less AI generated 
Focus on spacing format to be able to Copy and Paste directly 

Constraints: 
Keep it under 500 words 
Be straightforward, avoid sounding overly salesy
Use "~" rather than "-" as bulletpoints to make this ATS Friendly 
Do not do guessing work, only take what is from the interview notes
